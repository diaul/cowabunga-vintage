# TMNT Cards With Actual Vintage Tournament Play

## Overview

This analysis cross-references all cards from the TMNT sets against `vintage_played_cards.txt` — a dataset of every card that has appeared at least once in a published MTGO Vintage tournament decklist. The goal is to check how well the LLMs rated cards that have **proven** Vintage tournament history.

Out of 378 unique card names across the TMNT sets, **36 cards** have appeared in at least one Vintage tournament. These are mostly reprints of established staples (Brainstorm, Sol Ring, Underworld Breach) or format regulars (Dark Ritual, City of Brass, Arcbound Ravager).

This serves as a reality check: a good evaluator should rate these cards higher than average, since they have actual competitive pedigree.
